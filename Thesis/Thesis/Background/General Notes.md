This research captures fields in Computer Vision, Robotics and Photogrammetry.
- **Photogrammetry** is the primary field among the three. The result is relevant in Photogrammetry.
- **Computer Vision** accounts for the methodology for determining the poses since initial images are the ones being interpreted.
- **Robotics** through the use of kinematics to for camera movement.
- **Simulation & Virtual Reality:** Using Unity Engine to simulate the environment and camera movement ties into the fields of simulation and virtual reality.

The images that will be used to get geometric information about the object can be referred to as **Baseline** Images.
**Pose estimation** of the target object is relevant and serves as the first piece of information needed before other geometric details. This can give bounding box dimensions of the target object.
