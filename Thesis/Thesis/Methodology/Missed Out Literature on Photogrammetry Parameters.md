#literature #photogrammetry-factors

Came across a paper titled **# Accuracy Analysis for 3D Model Measurement Based on Digital Close-range Photogrammetry Technique for the Deep Foundation Pit Deformation Monitoring**.

That was after providing the keywords:
	"Working distance close-range photogrammetry"
The paper was on analysis of Deep Foundation Pits using Photogrammetry-a subject of interest in Civil Engineering.
The paper validates the use of photogrammetry for said analysis up to the sub-millimeter level in some cases.
According to the paper, accuracy is affected by:
	- Pixel Resolution ⇾ increase is better
	- Focal Length ⇾ increase is better
	- Photographic Distance(working distance) ⇾ increase is worse
	- Photographic baseline
> The ratio of photographic baseline to photographic distance (BDR) has a much greater effect on accuracy than other factors. The photographic distance with a resolution of less than 1.0mm and the BDR value of 0.6–1.8 are recommended.
