#capture #DOE
Initially, I had the following parameters to consider:
	1. Patterns
		- Circular
		- Spiral 
		- Chamfer Square
		- Random Circle
	2. Working Distance
		- Close
		- Medium
		- Far
	3. Camera Tilt
		- No Tilt
		- Proportional Tilt
The constraints of the Experiment are:
	- A single indoor scene 
	- The camera and lens of choice(in simulation) is a Raspberry PI IMX477 with the PT361060M3MP12

A factorial design was under consideration, but that would mean experiments at least 24 experiments
