#neural-networks #deep-learning 

In AI research, datasets are curated on which models/algorithms are trained/developed on/from. 
Datasets may target a subset or a specific field and it is up to the researcher to come up with models for that field. 
One such datasets is Sun RGB-D. It is a dataset containing 10,000 RGB-D images captured by 4 different sensors. It is an RGB-D Scene Understanding Benchmark Suite. The dataset has been used to come up with model in different fields including but not limited to: Semantic Segmentation, 3D Object Detection, Monocular 3D object detection, Room layout estimation etc.
