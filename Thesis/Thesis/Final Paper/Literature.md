2. A next best view method based on self-occlusion information in depth images for moving object
	1. Focuses on NBV for moving objects. Utilises depth maps. Self occlusions are modelled(space quadrilateral division, self-occlusion avoidance is computed a second depth image is take and points are matched with the first to get the motion of the object). "Finally, the next best view is determined by combining the result of self-occlusion avoidance and 3D motion estimation."
